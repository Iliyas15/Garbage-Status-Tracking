Swachh Bharat Monitoring the Garbage Collection is a campaign by OUR TEAM IS TO Monitoring THE GARBAGE FROM EACH HOUSE OF THE VILLAGE ,AND TO KEEP STREETS IN VILLAGE CLEAN . The campaign was officially launched on 2 OctoberIN GITAM UNIVERSITY BANGALORE It is India's FIRST ever drive with 3 million government employees, and especially school and college students from all parts of India, participating in the campaign.

Gitam exhorted people to fullfill Mahatma Gandhi's vision of Clean India. The green village movement that seeks to create a Clean India. Cleanliness was very close to Mahatma Gandhi's heart. A clean India is the best tribute we can pay to Bapu.
